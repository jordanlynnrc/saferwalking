What is Safer Walking?

Safer Walking is a GPS tracking service with people living with dementia in mind. A safeguarding measure for those who tend to wonder or get lost, providing reasurance to their families and reducing stress.
The service consists of a GPS tracker and companion app which will show the location of the tracker, updating every 5 minutes, the last month of location history, and set safezones so you can be alerted when the device enters or leaves an area you choose.
The device is also equipped with an SOS button that will send a unuique alert to all logged in devices, from here you can see where the SOS was sounded from and call the tracking device and have a 2 way conversation with the user.
